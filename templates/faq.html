Questions, answered

Everything about how ForgeCV builds and scores your resume.

What is ForgeCV?

ForgeCV turns your details - typed in, or read from an uploaded resume - into a polished, one-page, ATS-optimized resume and a tailored cover letter. It rewrites your content, scores it against a job, and exports clean PDF and DOCX files.

What does the ATS score mean?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software employers use to parse and rank resumes. ForgeCV shows two scores, both computed in code (never guessed by the AI):

  • Parse-ability - how cleanly an ATS can read the resume: contact details present, standard sections, single column, no tables or graphics.
  • Job-match - how well your resume covers the exact keywords and phrases in the job description you provide.

Why isn't my job-match always 100?

Because the score is honest. Job-match reflects how many of the job's exact keywords and phrases genuinely appear in your resume. We never pad it with fake matches. Pasting the real job description (or picking a sample) and letting ForgeCV reuse those exact phrases - where true - raises it legitimately.

How do you make it ATS-friendly?

  • Single-column layout (parses far more reliably than two-column).
  • Standard section headings and standard round bullets.
  • No tables, columns, graphics, icons, or header/footer text.
  • Exact job keywords used in context, with acronyms spelled out, e.g. "Machine Learning (ML)".
  • Quantified, action-verb bullets and consistent, parseable dates.
  • Real selectable text in both PDF and DOCX, kept to one page.

Upload or fill in manually - what's the difference?

Two modes. Upload reads your existing PDF or DOCX and auto-fills the fields so you can review and edit. Fill manually lets you type everything from scratch. Either way, the final resume is rewritten and structured the same.

How do I tailor it to a specific job?

Paste the job description into the "Target job description" box, or click a sample chip (AI/ML, Web Dev, Data Science, etc.). ForgeCV then reorders and rewrites content to match that role and scores against it.

What can I download?

Your résumé as PDF and DOCX, and - when you provide a job description - a tailored cover letter in both formats too.

Is my data stored?

No. Your details are processed only to build your resume during the request and are not stored after the page closes. Files are generated on the fly and streamed to you.

Why one page?

For students and early-career roles, a focused one-page resume is the standard recruiters expect, and dense single-page resumes parse cleanly. ForgeCV automatically compacts the layout to fit one page.

What's it built with?

Python and Flask, with Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) used only for language rewriting. Parsing, ATS scoring, and document generation are deterministic Python. Built by Amaan Khan.
